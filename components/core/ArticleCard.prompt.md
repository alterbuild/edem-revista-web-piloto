Standard article card for the magazine home grid. Composes `Badge`.

```jsx
<ArticleCard
  image="/assets/foto.jpg"
  tone="lanzadera"
  category="Radar Lanzadera"
  title="3 startups que revolucionan el puerto de Valencia"
  excerpt="Las apuestas del último cohorte…"
  meta="6 min"
/>
```

Hover lifts the card and zooms the image. Use `tone`/`category` to color-code the ecosystem section.
